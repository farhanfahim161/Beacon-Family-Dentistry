Get in Touch

Why are you called "The Gentle Dentist"?

Dr. Tapnio has earned the nickname "The Gentle Dentist" through his calm, compassionate approach that specifically addresses dental fear and anxiety. He specializes in creating a stress-free environment for children and anxious adult patients, using techniques that minimize discomfort and maximize comfort.

What financing options do you offer?

We are transparent about costs and offer flexible financing through Cherry. Patients can apply in just 60 seconds and pay for treatments over time, including 0% APR options. We believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care regardless of their financial situation.

How long have you been serving Jacksonville?

Dr. Tapnio and his team at Beacon Family Dentistry have been serving the Jacksonville community since 1983 - that's over 40 years of continuous service! We emphasize long-term patient relationships and consistent, high-quality oral health maintenance for generations of families.

Do you treat children and anxious patients?

Absolutely! Our practice is specifically geared toward children and anxious adult patients. Our gentle approach, calming environment, and patient communication techniques are designed to make dental visits a positive experience for even the most nervous patients.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive suite of services including family & cosmetic dentistry, specialized restorations (crowns, bridges, dentures), oral surgery (implants, extractions), orthodontics, aesthetic treatments (Therapeutic Botox), and teeth whitening. Most dental needs can be handled in our one convenient location.
